Agile Regulatory is a techno-enabled regulatory compliance consulting firm dedicated to delivering tailored, high-impact solutions to a diverse clientele. With a commitment to excellence, integrity, and client satisfaction, we bring expertise in various industries, including Electronics, Electrical, IT, Telecom, Steel and Iron, Chemical, Fertilizers, Textile, Footwear, Medical Devices, Drugs and Cosmetics etc. Agile regulatory majorly deals in BIS Registration, WPC Certificate, Legal Metrology, CDSCO Certification, State Pollution Control Board Consents (CTE/CTO), Waste Management Compliances like E-Waste, Plastic Waste, Solid Waste, Hazardous Waste, Battery Waste, Biomedical Waste, FSSAI, Trademark, ISO, GMP, FDA, Drug License, Trademark, EPR Registration, and Company Registration etc. Our client-centric approach, driven by a team of experienced professionals, ensures we listen, innovate, and produce tangible results that empower organizations to thrive in a rapidly changing world.
