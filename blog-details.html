How to check Hallmark Symbol on Gold Products

Gold purchase is not a minor affair so it is essential to confirm the purity of gold. The BIS of India mandates that all products containing gold must be hallmarked in the country. It authenticates the gold and also protects the buyers from fake items that may flood the market. Unfortunately, not everyone knows how this can be done. Follow the simple steps below to help you confirm BIS Hallmark Registration before investing in the manufacturer.

What is BIS Hallmarking?

BIS Hallmarking is a system created by the Bureau of Indian Standards, BIS to confirm golds purity. The process includes testing the gold at a BIS-approved center. Once approved, the centre stamps the hallmark on the item. This mark contains all the key details about the golds quality and origin.

Key Parts of a BIS Hallmark Symbol

The BIS hallmark symbol has five main parts. Each part gives specific information:

  1. BIS Logo: Shows the product is certified by the Bureau of Indian Standards.
  2. Purity Grade: Displays the golds purity in carats (e.g., 22K, 18K) and as a fineness number (e.g., 916 for 22K).
  3. Assaying and Hallmarking Centres Mark: Identifies the center where the gold was tested.
  4. Jewelers Mark: Identifies the jeweller who made or sold the item.
  5. Year of Marking: Indicates the year using a specific code (e.g., A for a certain year).

How to Check the Hallmark on Gold and Silver Jwellery

Follow these steps to check if your gold product is genuine:

  1. Find the Hallmark on the Item: Look for the hallmark on the golds surface. In most cases, it is engraved on the clasp, back, or inner band of jewellery.
  2. Check Each Part of the Hallmark: Ensure all five parts of the hallmark are present. Look for the BIS logo, grade of purity, marks, and year code.
  3. Confirm the Purity Grade: Compare the hallmarks purity grade with what the seller promised. For example, 22K gold should have 916, which means 91.6% purity.
  4. Use the BIS Care App: Download the BIS Care app on your smartphone. Scan or enter the hallmark number to verify the golds authenticity. The app shows details about the jeweller, the hallmarking center, and the certification.
  5. Ask the Seller for Help: Should you have any concerns, request the seller to clarify the hallmark. Trusted jewellers should provide documents and clarify details.

Common Myths about BIS Hallmarking

Lets clear up some common misunderstandings:

  1. Myth: Hallmarking Ensures 100% Purity.
    Truth: The hallmark confirms purity but doesnt guarantee the item is free of all impurities.
  2. Myth: All Gold and Silver Items Are Hallmarked.
    Truth: Not all gold items have a hallmark. Always buy from BIS-certified jewelers.
  3. Myth: Hallmarking is Expensive.
    Truth: Hallmarking costs very little and doesnt significantly increase the golds price.

Importance of BIS Hallmark Registration

BIS Registration ensures that jewelers and centers meet strict gold purity standards. Heres why it matters:

  1. Protects Buyers: BIS Certification prevents buyers from purchasing fake or low-quality gold.
  2. Promotes Transparency: It provides clear information about gold purity.
  3. Ensures Legal Compliance: Jewelers with BIS Registration follow government rules, building trust with customers.

How Do Jewelers Get a BIS Hammark License?

Jewelers need a BIS License to sell hallmarked gold. The steps include:

  1. Submit an Application: Jewelers must apply for BIS Registration with necessary documents.
  2. Inspection: BIS officials inspect the jewelers location for compliance.
  3. Approval: After passing the inspection, the jeweler receives a BIS License.

How to Check BIS Hallmark Logo Online

You can also check a jewelers BIS Registration online:

  1. Visit the official BIS website.
  2. Search for registered jewelers in your area to verify their authenticity.

Benefits of Buying Hallmarked Gold and Silver

Purchasing hallmarked gold comes with many advantages:

  1. Guaranteed Purity: The hallmark acquired through BIS Certificate ensures the golds purity.
  2. Easy Resale: Hallmarked gold is easier to sell in the market or even exchange.
  3. Legal Safety: It reduces the risk of disputes over quality.

Conclusion

Checking the hallmark on gold is essential to ensure a safe investment. Understanding BIS Hallmarking and BIS Registration helps you make informed purchases.
